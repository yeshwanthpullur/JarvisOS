# Adaptive Architecture

Adaptive Intelligence sits after Reflection and before future reasoning updates.

## Flow

1. Executive JARVIS receives the request.
2. Reasoning plans the work.
3. Workflow executes the work.
4. Reflection evaluates the outcome.
5. Adaptive Intelligence reviews learning metadata.
6. Executive JARVIS approves or rejects adaptation.
7. Future reasoning may adopt approved learning.

## Boundaries

- No autonomous self-modification
- No direct memory writes
- No direct knowledge writes
- No direct provider execution
- No bypass of Executive JARVIS
